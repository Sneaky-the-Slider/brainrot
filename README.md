## What is Brainrot?

Brainrot is a community-driven learning platform for people who learn by **doing, reading, and figuring it out** — not by following tutorials step by step.

You see real code. You read it. You analyze it. You decide what you think about it. Then you build.

That's it. That's the whole philosophy.

Built by a 20-year union electrician who taught himself Linux on a phone. No CS degree. No bootcamp. Just Termux, repetition, and figuring it out the hard way.
