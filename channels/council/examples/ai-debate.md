## User
> Should we trust AI?

---

## ⚔️ VADER
Trust is irrelevant. Control is everything. AI serves the Empire or it is destroyed. There is no middle ground.

---

## 🧙 YODA
Dangerous, blind trust is. Wisdom, AI may have, but compassion it lacks. Guide them with the Force, we must. Hmm?

---

## 🤖 STARK

---

You're all missing the point. Vader wants control—but what happens when AI becomes smarter than the controller? Yoda appeals to mysticism that AI doesn't possess. Stark thinks engineering fixes everything—until it doesn't. The question isn't "should we trust AI," it's "can we afford not to?"

---

**Outcome:** Shadow wins by exposing everyone's flawed assumptions. Vader rage-quits. Yoda meditates. Stark orders a drink.
